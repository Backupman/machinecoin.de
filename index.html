It's not just a coin. It's more like a movement.


The Machinecoin (3 letter short form often used in public: “MAC”) is a free and open source cryptocurrency completely available at GitHub that was created on the basis of Litecoin and Bitcoin. To simply get started you can either compile and configure the given source code by yourself or you can grab one of the following pre-compiled packages. It is completely up to you.
